5 Step Guide to Your Perfect Video

Step 1: Choose a Trend

Browse TikTok to find popular sounds, decide on the message you want to convey, and make sure your video has a clear focus.

Step 2: Script & Visuals

If your video includes dialogue, take the time to script what you'll say and plan out the camera angles you want to use in advance.

Step 3: Set the Scene

Use natural lighting or ring lights for the best results. Make sure you have a clean background that enhances your video.

Step 4: Record & Edit

Use a tripod to keep the camera stable. Edit with TikTok or an external app to make cuts and add transitions.

Step 5: Engage!

Engage with your audience by encouraging them to like, comment, and share your video. Responding to comments helps foster connection and build a loyal fanbase over time.
